About MNYC Healthcare

Transforming Healthcare Administration with Innovative Solutions

Welcome To MNYC Provider Solutions

MNYC Healthcare is a multifaceted Medical Billing Service provider with facilities in India. We cover all facets of medical billing services to companies throughout the United States.

We are a dynamic company with the ability to keep pace with the exponential growth of technology. Our key to success is knowledge-based foresight and aggressive adaptation of emerging technologies, enabling us to deliver cost-efficient and cutting-edge solutions to our customers.
